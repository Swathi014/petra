MARBLES

gorgeous stones

Marble is gorgeous stones that offer classic beauty

Consisting primarily of calcite and dolomite or a combination of both minerals. Marbles are easy maintain and can be re polished if you need to bring shine back. It is a porous material so if strong stain material is spilled on marble, it is recommended to wipe it quickly so that the marble does not absorb the stain. There is enormous amount of waste generated in cutting the marble tiles. Inserting marbles in floors is very much time consuming. Marble is weak to acidic products, such as tomato vinegar and lemon.

GRANITE

igneous rock

Granite is igneous rock of visible crystalline, formation and texture

. It is composed of feldspar and quartz with a small amount of mica and monor accessory minerals. Granite is a much harder stronger stone than marble. Granite easily resists scratches and damage from heat, making it an ideal material for kitchen counters both inside the home and outside. Granite counters will not scuff or discolor from everyday household activities. For cleaning, Just use a soft cloth to dust the surface, or wipe it down with plain water or a mild soap.
